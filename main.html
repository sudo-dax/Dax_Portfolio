One Planet

In an age where we have the power of all humantiy at our fingertips,
yet knowledge itself has been trivialised and taken for granted.
A time where we focus on public safety at the expense of freedom,
we must look to new innovative ways to engage society in open discussion to motivate action.
I believe with the magic of code we can replace and improve old structures which are currently failing us,
and help us re-connect to the world while we regain our humanity.

Paving the Way:

We live in challenging times, with a rapidly changing ecosystem and an even faster evolving culture, I believe the time is ripe for people everywhere to start to collaborate on projects that make life easier and more enjoyable for everyone. This is a time of paradoxes, a time of great struggle, yet a time of great abundance. Abundance of knowledge and resources, yet they have not yet found a way to travel to those who need it most. I believe that coding is the magic in the recipe of life that could bring the ingredients needed to make the world a place to thrive for all.
